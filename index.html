Welcome to My GitHub Page.

My interest for computers and programming started very early in life when my parents bought me a Sinclair ZX-90 X micro computer back when I was around 10-years-old. While most of my friends would be out and about playing soccer, hide-and-seek, and other games after school, I'd go home and spend all of my free hours teaching myself BASIC and learning how to create games and tools. Eventually I met other computer enthusiasts and we would get together every now and then to code whatever tickled our fancy.

Though I really enjoyed programming, getting my hands on books, magazines, or anything that would allow me to grow turned out to be extremely difficult and I eventually pursued a career in the science field, leading up to a B.S in Biochemistry in 1997... and that would have been the end of that if it wasn't for a new friend I met in the Summer of 1996 (hey Vinny!!!) who rekindled my passion for all computer related topics!

Here's a short list of some of my pet projects that were born out of my curiosity, need to scratch an itch or necessity for learning a new language. I list them here with the hope that some of it may be helpful to others some day:

  • BillReminder » Never forget to pay your bills again! [CODE]
  • BookWorm » Track and manage books you want to read. [CODE]
  • ChoppedPress » Split WordPress XML export files into smaller files to import into a new WordPress installations. [CODE]
  • ConaryRecipes » A Conary recipe browser. [CODE]
  • Pylyglot » Pylyglot is a web based tool for translators. It contains a database of messages and their translations into several different languages that one can search for suggestions. [CODE]
